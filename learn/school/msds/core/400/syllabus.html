400 Math for Modelers: Syllabus, Projects, Reflections


MSDS 400: Intelligent Systems and Robotics
Instructor: Philip M. Goldfeder, Ph.D.

Description: Review of fundamental concepts from calculus, linear algebra, and probability with a focus upon applications in statistics and predictive modeling. The topics covered will include systems of linear equations and matrices, linear programming, concepts of probability underlying both classical and Bayesian statistics, differential calculus and integration. This will be taught using a Just I n Time (JIT) approach.

- Build math models.

- Interpret math models.

- Evaluate math models.
